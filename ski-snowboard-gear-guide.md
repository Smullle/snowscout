## Shopping Tips

### Budget-Conscious Approach
- **Off-season shopping**: Buy gear in spring/summer for 30-50% off
- **Previous year models**: Last year's tech is still excellent
- **Package deals**: Many retailers offer jacket+pants combos
- **Rental first**: Rent equipment your first few times before investing

### Mid-Range Sweet Spot
- **Best value**: Usually offers 80% of high-end performance at 50% of the price
- **Durability**: Quality materials that last multiple seasons
- **Features**: Most practical features without premium branding

### High-End Investment
- **Worth it for**: Frequent riders (20+ days/season)
- **Best features**: Latest technology, lightest weight, longest warranties
- **Resale value**: Premium gear holds value better

### Safety Priorities
Invest more in:
1. **Helmet** - Don't compromise on head protection
2. **Boots** - Proper fit prevents injury and improves performance
3. **Goggles** - Clear vision is essential for safety
4. **Avalanche gear** - If going backcountry, buy quality beacons
